## üìã Abstract

**IntelAcad** is an AI-powered web platform designed to guide Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) students through their academic and career journey. It bridges the gap between classroom learning and industry readiness by integrating modules like **Academic Journey Navigator**, **Smart User Profile**, **Certification & Internship Hub**, **Mock Assessments**, and **Resume Grader**. 

The platform offers three login types‚Äî**Student**, **Admin**, and **Company**‚Äîenabling students to track progress, apply for opportunities, and receive recommendations, while companies and admins manage postings and analytics. By combining personalized insights, AI-based evaluation, and real-time academic tracking, IntelAcad empowers students to plan, progress, and achieve their professional goals effectively.

## Features

### For Students

#### Smart User Profile Module
- **Comprehensive Profile Creation** ‚Äî Maintain academic and professional details including education, skills, and achievements.
- **Dynamic Skill Tracking** ‚Äî Add and categorize skills with proficiency levels.
- **Academic Records Management** ‚Äî Store CGPA, semester-wise course details, and milestones.
- **Project Portfolio** ‚Äî Showcase projects with descriptions, technologies used, and GitHub links.
- **Experience Timeline** ‚Äî Record internships, part-time jobs, and volunteer work.
- **Profile Completeness Indicator** ‚Äî Visual meter showing profile strength.

#### Academic Journey Navigator Module
- **Semester-wise Planning** ‚Äî Visualize your academic plan over 4 years.
- **Course Roadmap** ‚Äî View recommended courses with prerequisites and electives.
- **Skill Development Path** ‚Äî Track skills aligned with industry trends each semester.
- **Progress Tracking** ‚Äî Track course completions, skills, and goals.
- **Goal Setting** ‚Äî Define academic or career objectives with reminders.
- **Personalized Recommendations** ‚Äî Get suggestions based on career paths.
- **Industry Alignment** ‚Äî Compare your progress to industry requirements.
- **Export Journey Map** ‚Äî Download your roadmap as a PDF or visual timeline.

#### Assessment Hub
- **Discover Assessments:** Browse 100+ curated challenges across Aptitude, DSA, OOP, OS, and DBMS
- **Track Progress:** Record completions with scores, notes, and difficulty levels
- **Visualize Growth:** Interactive charts showing domain distribution and performance metrics
- **Success Stories:** Get inspired by peers who landed jobs at FAANG companies
- **Leaderboards:** Compete with fellow students and track your ranking
- **Export Data:** Download your progress as CSV or PDF reports

#### Resume Builder
- **Professional Templates:** Choose from Classic, Executive, and Modern designs
- **ATS Optimization:** Built-in ATS checker with score breakdown and suggestions
- **Smart Parsing:** Upload existing resumes and auto-fill information
- **Comprehensive Sections:** Personal info, experience, education, projects, skills, certifications, languages, achievements
- **Real-time Preview:** See changes instantly as you edit
- **Multiple Exports:** Download as PDF or print directly
- **Save & Manage:** Store multiple resume versions for different applications

#### Internship Management
- **Advanced Search & Filters** ‚Äî Find roles by skills, work mode, company, or deadlines.
- **One-Click Applications** ‚Äî Apply instantly with a pre-filled profile.
- **Application Tracking Dashboard** ‚Äî Monitor your application statuses.
- **Company Insights** ‚Äî View culture, reviews, and selection criteria.
- **Internship Feedback System** ‚Äî Rate and review completed internships.

#### Certification Management
- **Certification Marketplace** ‚Äî Browse verified courses from Coursera, AWS, Google, and more.
- **Skill-Based Suggestions** ‚Äî Personalized certification recommendations.
- **Cost & Duration Filters** ‚Äî Choose certifications by budget and availability.
- **Progress Tracking** ‚Äî Track certification progress and completion.
- **Credential Verification** ‚Äî Use blockchain for certificate authenticity.
- **Peer Learning Groups** ‚Äî Join groups for collaborative learning.
- **Certification Feedback** ‚Äî Share and view difficulty ratings and experiences.


### For Admins

#### Dashboard & Analytics
- **Real-time Metrics:** Active students, verified companies, pending approvals, active internships
- **Visual Reports:** Line charts, pie charts, and bar graphs for data visualization
- **User Registration Trends:** Track growth over time
- **Career Goals Analysis:** Understand student aspirations
- **Application Insights:** Monitor application patterns and success rates
- **Export Reports:** Generate comprehensive JSON reports

#### Approval Management
- **Internship Postings:** Review and approve/reject internship opportunities
- **Company Verifications:** Verify company credentials and authenticity
- **Request Changes:** Provide feedback for improvements
- **Track History:** View approved and rejected items with reasons
- **Restore Capability:** Recover accidentally rejected items

#### Content & Notifications
- **Content Upload:** Share videos, documents, and images with students
- **Draft System:** Prepare content before publishing
- **Multi-channel Notifications:** Send via In-App, Email, and SMS
- **Template Library:** Reusable notification templates
- **Analytics:** Track delivery, open, and click rates
- **Targeted Messaging:** Send to specific cohorts or user groups

#### User Management
- **Student Management:** View, edit, and manage student accounts
- **Company Management:** Oversee company profiles and postings
- **Account Recovery:** Restore deleted accounts
- **Verification Status:** Track and update verification badges
- **Data Export:** Export user data as CSV for analysis

### For Companies

#### Company Profile & Onboarding
- **Detailed Registration** ‚Äî Create company profiles with full details.
- **Verification Process** ‚Äî Submit for admin approval to become verified.
- **Multi-User Access** ‚Äî Allow multiple team members with role-based access.
- **Dashboard Access** ‚Äî Manage recruitment and internships from one hub.

#### Internship Posting & Management
- **Easy Posting Interface** ‚Äî Post roles with rich descriptions and requirements.
- **Structured Forms** ‚Äî Define skills, duration, eligibility, and stipend.
- **Draft Management** ‚Äî Save roles as drafts or schedule them.
- **Posting Analytics** ‚Äî Measure performance by views and applications.

#### Application Management
- **Applicant Dashboard** ‚Äî See all applicants by post and application status.
- **Advanced Filtering** ‚Äî Sort by GPA, skills, and experience.
- **Communication Tools** ‚Äî Send interview calls and emails directly.
- **Interview Scheduling** ‚Äî Use the calendar to manage interviews.
- **Candidate Comparison** ‚Äî View applicants side-by-side.
- **Notes & Comments** ‚Äî Add internal notes for team review.
- **Export Options** ‚Äî Download data as CSV or PDF.

## üõ†Ô∏è Technology Stack

### Frontend
- **Framework:** [Next.js 14](https://nextjs.org/) (App Router)
- **Language:** [TypeScript](https://www.typescriptlang.org/)
- **Styling:** [Tailwind CSS v4](https://tailwindcss.com/)
- **UI Components:** [shadcn/ui](https://ui.shadcn.com/) (Radix UI primitives)
- **Charts:** [Recharts](https://recharts.org/)
- **Icons:** [Lucide React](https://lucide.dev/)
- **Notifications:** [Sonner](https://sonner.emilkowal.ski/)

### State Management
- **Local State:** React Hooks (useState, useEffect)
- **Routing:** Next.js App Router (useRouter, useSearchParams)
- **Storage:** localStorage (client-side persistence)

### File Handling
- **PDF Parsing:** PDF.js
- **DOCX Parsing:** Mammoth.js
- **File Upload:** Native HTML5 File API

### Development Tools
- **Package Manager:** npm / yarn / pnpm
- **Linting:** ESLint
- **Formatting:** Prettier
- **Version Control:** Git & GitHub

### Deployment
- **Platform:** Vercel
- **CI/CD:** GitHub Actions
- **Domain:** Custom domain support
